Renting and Shared living

  • Renting process

    Here is a general guide on the renting process in Melbourne. Renters have rights. You can read about your rights on Consumer Affairs Victoria website.

    Remember to keep the house clean. Having a good track record and reference will go a long way and saves your a lot of hussle down the road. But this does not mean you have to give unreasonable concessions to the landlord. If your right under the law and contract is violated, act to protect your right.
